Rahiz — born Celso de Jesus Morais, Lisboa, 1980 — is a Luso-Cape Verdean author, composer, producer & host. Grand-nephew of the late Luís Morais.

Formerly known as Celso OPP, he opened a new chapter in 2014 from France, fusing hip-hop, reggae, kizomba, afrobeat and zouk — most of it sung in Cape Verdean Creole.
